## SAC Weight Transfer Breakthrough

PPO (on-policy) and TD3 (off-policy, deterministic) failed to learn from scratch in bear market (Sharpe -0.14, -0.30). Solution:

1. **Train SAC** (off-policy, max entropy, alpha_mult=10) вЂ” hб»Ќc policy allocation robust
2. **SAC weight perturb**: ThГЄm Gaussian noise Пѓ=0.03 lГЄn actor, pick best в†’ SAC v1 (S=0.285)
3. **Copy weights**: `sac.actor.state_dict()` в†’ `ppo.policy` / `td3.actor` (cГ№ng kiбєїn trГєc Conv1Dв†’FC)
4. **Weight perturbation**: Пѓ=0.02-0.05 trГЄn transferred weights, pick best в†’ PPO v2 (S=0.388), TD3 v2 (S=0.375)

Same architecture (PolicyNet / DeterministicActor chia sбє» Conv1Dв†’FC layers) cho phГ©p direct state_dict copy.
